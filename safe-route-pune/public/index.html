Features

Everything you need to travel safer

Combining historical records with live crowd intelligence to give you the clearest picture of safety on Pune's roads.

ML Safety Model

Trained on Pune's accident blackspots, NCRB crime data, PMC lighting records and women-safety reports. Updated as crowd feedback arrives.

Three Route Modes

Choose Fastest (Google ETA), Safest (highest safety score), or Balanced — a weighted blend of both.

Crowd-sourced Intelligence

After each journey, users rate safety along their route. Negative reports lower area scores; positive ones reinforce safe corridors.

Live Safety Heatmap

Red clusters = danger zones. Green = safe areas. Overlaid directly on the map, updated with every crowd-sourced data point.

Women Safety Layer

Dedicated data layer for incidents against women — isolated stretches, poor lighting, and time-of-day risk patterns across Pune.

Journey History

All your past routes, safety scores and feedback in one place. Track how areas improve over time (signed-in users only).

Process

How SafeRoute works

Four steps from opening the app to arriving safely.

01

Enter your journey

Type origin and destination anywhere in Pune. The autocomplete uses the Google Places API.

02

AI scores every route

Our model samples 20+ waypoints per route, checks each against 34 safety zones plus live crowd data, and produces a safety score.

03

Pick Fastest / Safest / Balanced

Compare all three options side-by-side with their safety score, ETA and distance before choosing.

04

Rate & improve the model

After your journey, a one-time feedback prompt lets you flag unsafe spots. Your report immediately shifts the heatmap for everyone.
